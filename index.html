About Me

Hello! My name is Mehetab and I am the creator of CrazeStudio. I enjoy building software, web applications, Minecraft tools, resource packs, launchers and experimental projects.

Most of my projects are developed using mobile devices while continuously learning HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python and Android development technologies.

This website serves as the official portfolio and project showcase for CrazeStudio where visitors can explore my creations and follow future development updates.

About CrazeLauncher

CrazeLauncher is one of my primary projects. It is a Minecraft launcher focused on creating a modern experience with a clean interface and future expansion possibilities.

The project aims to simplify Minecraft management while providing easy access to launcher features and customization options.

About Ultra Low Fire

Ultra Low Fire is a Minecraft resource pack designed to improve visibility by reducing the visual obstruction caused by fire effects during gameplay.

The pack is lightweight and intended for players who prefer a cleaner gameplay experience.
